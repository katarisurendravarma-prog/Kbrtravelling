The Golden Path – Buddhist Circuit

Follow in the footsteps of Lord Buddha through India's sacred spiritual sites

This 13-day spiritual journey takes you across India and Nepal to key sites in the life and teachings of Lord Buddha — from Lumbini to Bodh Gaya to Sarnath — while experiencing Buddhist culture, rituals, and serene monasteries.

📍 Destinations Covered

Delhi – Lucknow – Shravasti – Lumbini – Kushinagar – Vaishali – Nalanda – Rajgir – Bodh Gaya – Varanasi – Delhi

🗓️ Duration

13 Days / 12 Nights

📅 Best Time to Visit

Available year-round, especially serene from October to March

🕉️ Tour Highlights

  • Explore historic Delhi including Qutub Minar and Lodhi Gardens
  • Visit Lumbini, the birthplace of Lord Buddha
  • Pay homage at Kushinagar’s Mahapari Nirvana Temple
  • See Sarnath where Buddha gave his first sermon
  • Discover Nalanda’s ancient university ruins
  • Walk the sacred grounds of Bodh Gaya and Sujata Village

📝 Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive in Delhi

Traditional welcome and hotel check-in. Evening at leisure.

Day 2: Delhi Exploration

Visit Qutub Minar, Gandhi Smriti, Lodhi Gardens, and Ladakh Buddhist Vihar monastery.

Day 3: Day Trip to Agra

Excursion to visit the Taj Mahal and Agra Fort. Return to Delhi by evening.

Day 4: Delhi – Lucknow – Shravasti

Train or flight to Lucknow, then drive to Shravasti. Overnight stay.

Day 5: Shravasti – Lumbini

Explore Maheth and Saheth before heading to Lumbini.

Day 6: Lumbini – Kushinagar

Visit Mayadevi Temple in Lumbini, then proceed to Kushinagar.

Day 7: Kushinagar – Vaishali

Morning visit to Mahapari Nirvana Temple and Ramabhar Stupa. Drive to Vaishali.

Day 8: Vaishali – Bodhgaya via Rajgir & Nalanda

Explore Nalanda and Rajgir’s historical Buddhist sites. Overnight in Bodhgaya.

Day 9: Bodhgaya

Visit Mahabodhi Temple, Niranjana River and Sujata Village.

Day 10: Bodhgaya

Excursion to Dungeshwari Caves and Barbara Hill Caves. Return to Bodhgaya.

Day 11: Bodhgaya – Varanasi

Drive to Varanasi. Evening boat ride and Ganga Aarti.

Day 12: Varanasi

Morning boat ride and city tour. Afternoon excursion to Sarnath.

Day 13: Varanasi – Delhi

Fly to Delhi. Optional city tour based on your flight timing. Tour ends.
