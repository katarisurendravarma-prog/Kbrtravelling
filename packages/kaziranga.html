Manas, Nameri & Kaziranga Wildlife Tour

Explore Northeast India's wild wonders across three iconic national parks

Join the 14-day Wildlife Expedition through the dense mangroves of Sunderban, the rhino habitats of Kaziranga, and the foothills of Manas & Nameri for a thrilling eco-adventure in India’s northeast.

Book Now

📍 Destinations Covered

Kolkata – Sunderban – Guwahati – Manas – Nameri – Kaziranga – Kolkata

🗓️ Duration

14 Days / 13 Nights

📅 Best Time to Visit

November to April – Ideal weather for jungle safaris and boat rides.

🦏 Tour Highlights

  • Cruise through Sunderban’s mangrove delta
  • Spot Bengal tigers & crocodiles at watchtowers
  • Jeep safaris in Manas and Kaziranga
  • Trekking and river rafting at Nameri National Park
  • Sunset cruise on the Brahmaputra River

📝 Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Kolkata

Meet at Kolkata Airport and transfer to hotel. Overnight in Kolkata.

Day 2: Kolkata – Sunderban

Drive to Basanti, then boat to Sajnekhali. Visit Sudhanyakhali Watchtower. Overnight in forest camp/lodge.

Day 3: Sunderban National Park

Boat safari through Sunderban, visiting Burirdabri or Netidhopani towers. Explore crocodile farm. Overnight stay.

Day 4: Sunderban – Kolkata – Guwahati

Return to Kolkata and fly to Guwahati. Transfer to hotel.

Day 5: Guwahati – Manas National Park

Drive to Manas at the foothills of the Himalayas. Afternoon jungle safari. Overnight in forest resort.

Day 6: Manas Tiger Reserve

Morning and evening jeep safaris. Spot rare wildlife and enjoy nature. Overnight in resort.

Day 7: Manas – Guwahati

Return drive to Guwahati. Enjoy sunset Brahmaputra cruise. Overnight in Guwahati.

Day 8: Guwahati – Nameri National Park

Drive to Nameri, check-in at camp. Evening walk and local exploration. Overnight in camp.

Day 9: Nameri Exploration

Morning guided forest trek. Afternoon rafting in Jia Bhoroli River. Overnight in camp.

Day 10: Nameri – Kaziranga

Drive to Kaziranga. Afternoon jeep safari in eastern range. Overnight in resort.

Day 11: Kaziranga National Park

Morning and afternoon safaris through Kaziranga’s central and western zones. Overnight stay.

Day 12: Kaziranga Exploration

Continue jeep safaris and wildlife sightings. Optional elephant safari. Overnight in resort.

Day 13: Kaziranga – Kolkata

Early safari, breakfast, drive to Guwahati. Fly to Kolkata. Overnight in hotel.

Day 14: Departure from Kolkata

After breakfast, transfer to airport for your onward flight.
