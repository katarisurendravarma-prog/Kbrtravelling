📅 Best Time to Visit

  • September to November: Clear skies & great views
  • December to January: Chill weather & snow nearby
  • February to June: Perfect for sightseeing & blooms

🌄 Tour Overview

Darjeeling offers colonial charm, iconic tea estates, and Toy Train joyrides. Gangtok adds a dash of spirituality with monasteries, vibrant streets, and nature walks.

📸 Major Attractions

  • Darjeeling: Tiger Hill sunrise, Tea Garden, HMI, Zoological Park, Toy Train
  • Gangtok: Rumtek Monastery, Tashi View Point, Orchid Sanctuary, Enchey Monastery

📝 Tour Itinerary

Day 01: Arrive in Delhi

Meet our representative and transfer to hotel. Evening free.

Day 02: Delhi – Bagdogra (Flight) – Darjeeling

Fly to Bagdogra and drive to Darjeeling (3 hrs). Overnight stay.

Day 03: Darjeeling Sightseeing

Visit Tiger Hill, Ghoom Monastery, Tea Gardens, HMI, Zoo, and local market.

Day 04: Darjeeling – Gangtok

Drive to Gangtok. Visit Enchey Monastery, Tashiling, White Hall & Market.

Day 05: Explore Gangtok

Excursion to Tashi View, Rumtek Monastery, Namgyal Institute, Orchid Garden.

Day 06: Gangtok – Bagdogra – Delhi

Drive to Bagdogra and fly back to Delhi. Check into hotel.

Day 07: Departure

Transfer to airport. End of the tour with beautiful memories.
