📍 Destinations Covered

Delhi – Rishikesh – Uttarkashi – Gangotri – Rudraprayag – Badrinath – Joshimath – Kausani – Nainital – Corbett

🗓️ Duration

12 Days / 11 Nights

📅 Best Time to Visit

April to June & September to October

🌄 Tour Highlights

  • Rishikesh: Lakshman Jhula, Shivanand Ashram, Ganga Aarti
  • Uttarkashi: Vishwanath Temple, Bhagirathi River
  • Gangotri: Gangotri Temple, Harsil Valley, Hot Springs
  • Badrinath: Badrinath Temple, Mana Village
  • Kausani: Himalayan panoramic views
  • Nainital: Naini Lake, Mall Road, Colonial charm
  • Jim Corbett: Wildlife safari at India's first national park

📝 Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive Delhi

Meet our representative at the airport and transfer to hotel. Overnight in Delhi.

Day 2: Delhi – Rishikesh

Drive to Rishikesh via Haridwar. Attend the sacred Ganga Aarti in the evening.

Day 3: Rishikesh – Uttarkashi

Proceed to Uttarkashi. Visit the Vishwanath Temple. Overnight stay.

Day 4: Uttarkashi – Gangotri – Uttarkashi

Day excursion to the Gangotri Temple and Harsil Valley. Return in the evening.

Day 5: Uttarkashi – Rudraprayag

Drive to Rudraprayag, the sacred confluence of Alaknanda and Mandakini rivers.

Day 6: Rudraprayag – Badrinath

Scenic mountain drive to Badrinath via Joshimath. Visit the Badrinath Temple.

Day 7: Badrinath – Joshimath – Auli

Explore Auli's snow-capped beauty before overnight stay at Joshimath.

Day 8: Joshimath – Kausani

Drive through lush forests and valleys to reach peaceful Kausani.

Day 9: Kausani – Almora – Nainital

Stop at Almora’s Nanda Devi Temple before arriving in lake-town Nainital.

Day 10: Nainital – Jim Corbett

Head to Corbett National Park for jungle safari and wildlife adventures.

Day 11: Jim Corbett

Enjoy morning and evening safaris. Spot tigers, elephants, and rich birdlife.

Day 12: Corbett – Delhi

Return to Delhi and transfer to airport for departure. Tour concludes.
