Chardham Yatra Tour

Seek divine blessings from the Himalayas' four sacred shrines

The Chardham Yatra — encompassing Yamunotri, Gangotri, Kedarnath, and Badrinath — is among the holiest spiritual journeys for Hindus. Travel through the majestic Himalayas for blessings, peace, and salvation.

📍 Destinations Covered

Delhi – Haridwar – Barkot – Yamunotri – Uttarkashi – Gangotri – Guptkashi – Gaurikund – Kedarnath – Rudraprayag – Badrinath – Rishikesh – Delhi

🗓️ Duration

11 Days / 10 Nights

📅 Best Time to Visit

May to October

🕉️ Overview

This life-changing pilgrimage brings you face-to-face with the divine. From temple treks and glacier-fed rivers to spiritual towns and mountain serenity, the Chardham Yatra is an experience of a lifetime.

📝 Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival Delhi – Drive to Haridwar

Evening Ganga Aarti or rest at leisure.

Day 2–3: Barkot & Yamunotri

Drive to Barkot and trek to Yamunotri for temple visit and return.

Day 4–5: Uttarkashi & Gangotri Excursion

Visit the origin of the Ganga and return to Uttarkashi.

Day 6–7: Guptkashi – Kedarnath Trek

Trek to Kedarnath and visit the Jyotirlinga temple of Lord Shiva.

Day 8–9: Rudraprayag – Badrinath

Drive to Badrinath and visit the temple and hot springs.

Day 10: Rishikesh Visit

Stop at Devprayag and spend the night in Rishikesh.

Day 11: Return to Delhi

Transfer to the airport with blessings and memories.
