Complete India Tour

Embark on a 24-day journey across India's spiritual, cultural, and scenic landscapes

This Complete India Tour is an immersive cultural journey across iconic cities and spiritual landmarks from the north to the south of India, culminating in the vibrant metropolis of Mumbai.

📍 Destinations Covered

Delhi – Agra – Khajuraho – Varanasi – Kolkata – Mahabalipuram – Pondicherry – Thanjavur – Madurai – Periyar – Alleppey – Cochin – Goa – Mumbai

🗓️ Duration

24 Days / 23 Nights

📅 Best Time to Visit

October to March — Ideal for pleasant weather and festival experiences.

🌍 Tour Highlights

  • Witness the Taj Mahal at sunrise
  • Explore Khajuraho's erotic temples
  • Experience Ganga Aarti in Varanasi
  • Visit French-era towns of Pondicherry
  • Stay in a houseboat in Kerala's backwaters
  • Enjoy beaches and heritage in Goa

📝 Itinerary (Highlights)

Day 1–2: Delhi

Arrival and exploration of Old and New Delhi, including Red Fort, Humayun’s Tomb, and Qutub Minar.

Day 3–4: Agra & Jhansi/Orchha

Visit the Taj Mahal, Agra Fort, then train ride to Jhansi and travel to Khajuraho via Orchha.

Day 5–6: Khajuraho to Varanasi

Explore temples and fly to Varanasi. Witness Ganga Aarti and take a sunrise boat ride.

Day 7–9: Kolkata & Mahabalipuram

Colonial-era Kolkata highlights, then travel to Mahabalipuram for ancient rock-cut temples.

Day 10–14: Pondicherry, Thanjavur, Madurai

Tour ashrams, temples, and palaces; experience South India’s sacred heartlands.

Day 15–18: Periyar, Alleppey, and Cochin

Spice plantations, wildlife cruise, traditional dance and cooking classes.

Day 19–21: Goa

Leisure at beaches and heritage churches of North and South Goa.

Day 22–24: Mumbai

Explore Elephanta Caves, Gateway of India, and colonial art deco buildings.
