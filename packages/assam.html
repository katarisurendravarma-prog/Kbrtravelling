Assam & Sundarbans Cruise Tour

Wildlife, culture, and river adventure across northeast India

This 15-day immersive journey explores the breathtaking Assamese countryside, tribal heritage, and the mystical Sundarbans mangrove forests through guided tours, safaris, and a luxury cruise experience.

📍 Destinations Covered

Kolkata – Jorhat – Majuli – Kaziranga – Tezpur – Barpeta – Manas – Guwahati – Kolkata (via Sundarbans Cruise)

🗓️ Duration

15 Days / 14 Nights

📅 Best Time to Visit

October to April

🌿 Overview

From tribal monasteries of Majuli and rhino safaris in Kaziranga, to tiger spotting in Manas and river cruising through the Sundarbans, this journey is packed with unforgettable adventures.

📝 Detailed Itinerary

Day 1–2: Arrival & Explore Kolkata

Visit Flower Market, ferry to artist quarters, Victoria Memorial, and College Street.

Day 3–4: Fly to Jorhat – Ferry to Majuli Island

Explore Satra monasteries, pottery villages, and tribal communities.

Day 5–6: Drive to Kaziranga & Wildlife Safaris

Experience elephant rides and jeep safaris in the UNESCO-listed park.

Day 7–8: Manas National Park

Tiger reserve exploration and visit to Bodo tribal village with cultural show.

Day 9–10: Guwahati & Kamakhya Temple – Fly to Kolkata

Evening in Guwahati and sacred temple visit before flight.

Day 11–14: Sundarbans Cruise (Vivada Cruises)

Luxury river cruise with wildlife excursions, island visits, and live performances.

Day 15: Return to Kolkata

Transfer to the airport for onward travel.

Your journey ends with memories of riverfront adventures, culture, and wildlife
