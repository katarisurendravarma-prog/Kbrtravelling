🗺️ West & North India Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Delhi

Meet our representative at IGI airport and transfer to the hotel. Overnight stay in Delhi.

Day 2: Delhi

City tour of Old & New Delhi. Includes Rickshaw ride, Jama Masjid, Qutab Minar, Gandhi Smriti, India Gate & more.

Day 3: Delhi – Agra

Drive to Agra. Visit the Taj Mahal, Agra Fort and local markets.

Day 4: Agra – Jaipur

En route visit Fatehpur Sikri, the Ghost City. Arrive Jaipur and relax.

Day 5: Jaipur

Tour Amber Fort, City Palace, Hawa Mahal and Jantar Mantar.

Day 6: Jaipur – Pushkar

Visit Brahma Temple, Ghats, Gurudwaras and Pushkar Lake.

Day 7: Pushkar – Udaipur

Drive to romantic Udaipur and spend the evening at leisure.

Day 8: Udaipur

Visit City Palace, Jagdish Temple, Saheliyon Ki Bari and enjoy a boat ride on Lake Pichola.

Day 9–10: Udaipur – Ahmedabad (via Statue of Unity)

Explore Gandhi Ashram, Sabarmati riverfront, Sidi Saiyyad Mosque and more.

Day 11: Ahmedabad – Jamnagar

Visit Bala Hanuman Temple and enjoy Ramdhun chanting experience.

Day 12: Jamnagar – Dwarka

See Lakhota Lake and Museum, then proceed to Dwarka.

Day 13–14: Dwarka – Somnath – Diu

Visit Dwarkadheesh Temple, Gomti Ghat, Somnath Temple, and Diu Fort.

Day 15: Diu

Relax at Nagoa Beach or explore the Portuguese heritage.

Day 16–17: Diu – Sasan Gir

See Asiatic lions and learn about conservation at Gir National Park.

Day 18–19: Gondal – Bhavnagar (via Palitana)

Visit vintage car museum and Palitana Jain Temples.

Day 20: Bhavnagar – Ahmedabad – Delhi

Fly to Delhi and transfer to hotel.

Day 21: Depart Delhi

After breakfast, transfer to airport for onward flight.
