Explore Jaisalmer – The Golden City of Rajasthan

A desert dream with forts, dunes, and timeless beauty

🏜️ Welcome to Jaisalmer

Nestled in the heart of the Thar Desert, Jaisalmer glows with yellow sandstone architecture and echoes of valor and royalty. Often called the “Golden City,” it is a magnificent blend of heritage and culture.

🌟 Top Attractions

  • Jaisalmer Fort: A UNESCO site built from yellow sandstone, also known as Sonar Quila.
  • Gadsisar Lake: A peaceful retreat with boats and stunning architecture.
  • Patwon Ki Haveli: A cluster of five intricately designed havelis showcasing Rajasthani craftsmanship.
  • Sam Sand Dunes: Desert safaris, sunset views, and the vibrant Desert Festival in February.

📅 Best Time to Visit

Plan your trip between December and February for cool weather. Avoid peak summer months due to extreme desert heat.

🎉 Top Activities

  • Camel Safari: Sunset rides across the golden dunes
  • Boating at Gadsisar Lake: Relax and enjoy peaceful views
  • Parasailing: Adventure above the desert plains

🚗 How to Reach

By Road: Jodhpur (300 km) is the nearest major city with buses and taxis available.
By Train: Jaisalmer Railway Station connects via limited routes.
By Air: Jaisalmer Airport offers seasonal flights from Jaipur and Jodhpur.
