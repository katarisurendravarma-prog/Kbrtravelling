Darjeeling – Queen of the Hills

Tea gardens, toy trains, and Himalayan vistas await you

🌄 Overview of Darjeeling

Darjeeling is a scenic hill station in West Bengal, nestled in the Eastern Himalayas. Known for its tea estates, British charm, and Himalayan views, Darjeeling offers a serene getaway year-round. It’s a perfect mix of nature, culture, and adventure.

🚂 Darjeeling Himalayan Railway

The famous Toy Train, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, takes you through lush hills and charming towns, offering a nostalgic ride through Darjeeling’s magical landscape.

🏞️ Best Places to Visit

  • 🏔️ Tiger Hill – Witness a stunning sunrise over Kanchenjunga
  • 🚞 Batasia Loop – A spiral railway loop with gardens and war memorial
  • 🧘 Peace Pagoda – Serene spot showcasing Buddhist teachings
  • 🐾 Himalayan Zoological Park – Home to snow leopards and red pandas
  • 🏛️ Himalayan Mountaineering Institute – Museum and training center for mountain lovers

📅 Best Time to Visit

March to June and October to February offer pleasant weather. Summer is great for sightseeing, while winters bring snow and crisp mountain air.

🎉 Festivals & Events

  • Durga Puja – Cultural celebration in September/October
  • Darjeeling Carnival – Music, dance, and local food (November)
  • Buddha Jayanti – Celebrated in local monasteries (April/May)
  • Tihar – Festival of Lights, similar to Diwali

🛤️ How to Reach

By Air: Bagdogra Airport (70 km); taxi or bus to Darjeeling
By Train: Nearest major station is New Jalpaiguri (NJP); toy train or taxi to Darjeeling
By Road: Well connected to Siliguri, Kolkata, and other cities

📍 Nearby Places

  • Kalimpong – Colonial charm and monasteries
  • Mirik – Lake, gardens, and orange orchards
  • Kurseong – Peaceful town with tea estates
  • Siliguri – Base town for travel and shopping
  • Sandakphu – Trekking haven with Himalayan views
