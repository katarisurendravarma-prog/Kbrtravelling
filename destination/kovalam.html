Relax by the Arabian Sea in Kovalam

Golden sands, swaying palms, and tranquil Ayurvedic bliss await in Kerala’s coastal gem

🌴 Overview of Kovalam

Located on Kerala's southwest coast, Kovalam is a serene beach destination offering a harmonious blend of relaxation and cultural experiences. Famous for its crescent-shaped beaches, wellness retreats, and local seafood, it's a favorite for couples, families, and solo travelers alike.

🌅 Why Visit Kovalam?

Kovalam combines leisure with culture—from sunbathing at Lighthouse Beach to Ayurvedic massages, spice-scented streets, and traditional temple festivals. It’s a perfect escape to soak in nature and rejuvenate your soul.

📸 Best Places to Visit

  • Lighthouse Beach – Iconic lighthouse, golden sands, and sunset views
  • Hawah Beach – Peaceful, palm-lined shores with local fishermen at work
  • Samudra Beach – Less crowded, ideal for a tranquil getaway
  • Vizhinjam Marine Aquarium – Educational and fun for families
  • Halcyon Castle – Historic palace-turned-hotel reflecting royal heritage

🌤️ Best Time to Visit

Winter (Oct–Feb): Pleasant weather for beaches and sightseeing
Summer (Mar–May): Hot and humid; Ayurveda treatments recommended
Monsoon (Jun–Sep): Greenery and rain; offbeat travel experience

🎉 Festivals to Experience

  • Onam – Kerala's grand festival with music, dance, and floral decorations
  • Kovalam Carnival – Beach parades, cultural shows, and seafood feasts

🎒 Things to Do in Kovalam

  • Get an Ayurvedic massage by the sea
  • Surf or swim at Lighthouse Beach
  • Explore Vizhinjam Rock Cut Cave Temple
  • Shop for local crafts and spices
  • Take a catamaran ride on the Arabian Sea

🛬 How to Reach Kovalam

By Air: Trivandrum International Airport (15 km)
By Train: Trivandrum Central Railway Station (15 km)
By Road: NH66 connects Kovalam with major cities; buses and taxis from Trivandrum

📍 Places to Visit Nearby

  • Varkala – Cliff beaches and Janardanaswamy Temple (60 km)
  • Trivandrum – Museums, temples, and zoo (15 km)
  • Poovar Island – Backwaters and floating cottages (18 km)
  • Kanyakumari – Southernmost tip of India and sunrise point (90 km)
  • Ponmudi – Lush green hill station ideal for trekking (60 km)
