Mandawa – The Open-Air Art Gallery of Rajasthan

Explore haveli heritage and desert charm

Mandawa, in the Shekhawati region of Rajasthan, is famous for its ornately painted havelis and rich frescoes. Once a key trading post, Mandawa has evolved into an artistic open-air museum preserving Rajasthan’s cultural brilliance through murals and architecture.

📅 Best Time to Visit

October to March is the best season to visit Mandawa, with pleasant temperatures ranging from 10°C to 25°C ideal for walking tours and sightseeing.

🏛️ Top Attractions

  • Murmuria Haveli: Built in the 1930s, known for impressive frescoes and architecture.
  • Hanuman Prasad Goenka Haveli: Features divine murals of Lord Shiva, Indra, and others.
  • Harlalka Well: A scenic stepwell with minarets and camel ramp nearby.

🎨 Things to Do

  • Take a walking tour of the havelis and admire Mandawa’s street art
  • Enjoy camel rides near Binsidhar and Saraf Havelis
  • Visit Nawalgarh nearby, known for similar Shekhawati artwork

🚗 How to Reach

By Road: 240 km from Delhi, 170 km from Jaipur – well connected via NH-11.

🚉 Nearest Railway Station

Jhunjhunu – 30 km from Mandawa, with taxi and bus services available.

✈️ Nearest Airport

Jaipur International Airport – about 150 km away.
