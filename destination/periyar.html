Periyar – The Untamed Wilderness of Kerala

Home to lush forests, wild tigers, and the magical backwaters of Thekkady

Located in the Western Ghats of Kerala, Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary is one of India’s most captivating eco-tourism destinations. From the Bengal tiger to tranquil backwaters and spice plantations, it’s an immersive nature experience set in Thekkady.

📅 Best Time to Visit

Periyar is open all year round, but the best months are September to April when the weather is ideal for safaris, hiking, and nature tours.

🐅 Top Attractions in Periyar

  • Periyar Tiger Reserve: Home to Bengal tigers, elephants, wild boars, and unique birdlife
  • Mangla Devi Temple, Kurikaddy & Chellarkovil: Spiritual, scenic, and eco-tourism spots
  • Kathakali & Kalari Shows: Traditional performances at Mudra & Kadathanadan Kalari Centres
  • Anakkara Village: Known for its spice plantations and waterfalls
  • Organic Farms: Guided tours of cardamom, cinnamon, and medicinal herb plantations

🎒 Things to Do in Periyar

  1. Boat Safari: Spot elephants and birds while cruising Periyar Lake
  2. Bamboo Rafting: Eco-adventure through forested waters
  3. Border Hiking: Trek with forest guides along remote trails
  4. Kayaking in Gavi: Paddle through serene jungle waterways

🚗 How to Reach

  • By Road: Nearest hub is Kumily, connected to Madurai, Ernakulam, and Kottayam
  • By Train: Kottayam Railway Station – 114 km away
  • By Air: Madurai Airport (140 km) or Cochin International Airport (190 km)
