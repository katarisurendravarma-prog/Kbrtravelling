Discover the Snowy Wonderland of Gulmarg

Ski slopes, meadows of flowers, and Himalayan adventures await in Kashmir’s gem

❄️ Overview of Gulmarg

Set in the Pir Panjal range of Jammu & Kashmir, Gulmarg is a dreamy retreat for those who seek natural beauty, thrilling adventures, or peaceful escapes. From snow-covered slopes in winter to blooming wildflower meadows in summer, Gulmarg enchants in every season.

🎢 Why Visit Gulmarg?

Gulmarg is home to the famous Gulmarg Gondola—one of Asia’s highest cable cars—and world-class ski slopes. Add to that the charm of Kashmiri hospitality, local cuisine, wooden cottages, and Sufi heritage, and you have a destination like no other.

📸 Best Places to Visit

  • Gulmarg Gondola – Asia’s highest cable car with snow-capped views
  • Khilanmarg – A panoramic meadow perfect for treks and photo ops
  • Alpather Lake – Pristine high-altitude lake frozen until June
  • Apharwat Peak – Accessible via Gondola Phase 2; skier’s paradise
  • St. Mary’s Church – Quaint 1900s chapel set in pine forests

🌤️ Best Time to Visit

Winter (Dec–Feb): Snow sports like skiing, snowboarding, and snow cycling
Summer (Apr–Jun): Ideal for gondola rides, trekking, golf, and wildflowers
Monsoon (Jul–Sep): Quiet, lush, and less crowded—perfect for nature lovers

🎉 Festivals to Experience

  • Winter Sports Festival – Annual event with snowboarding and snow cycling
  • Tulip Festival (Srinagar) – Held in April; adds color to your Kashmir trip

🎒 Things to Do in Gulmarg

  • Ride the Gulmarg Gondola – A surreal ascent above snowy peaks
  • Ski or snowboard at Apharwat – A thrill for snow lovers
  • Trek to Alpather Lake – Scenic and peaceful trail
  • Sledging and snow play – Great for kids and families
  • Golf at Gulmarg Golf Course – Among the world’s highest
  • Stroll through wildflower meadows – A romantic summer activity
  • Click photos at Khilanmarg – Picture-perfect Himalayan backdrop

⛷️ Adventure Activities

  • Skiing and Snowboarding – Powdery slopes for all skill levels
  • Trekking – Trails to Khilanmarg and Alpather Lake
  • Camping – Under starry skies in alpine forests
  • Gondola Ride – Second-highest cable car in the world
  • Snowmobiling – Zoom across snowy plains
  • Heli-skiing – Extreme skiing dropped from helicopters

🛬 How to Reach Gulmarg

By Air: Srinagar Airport (50 km) connects with Delhi, Mumbai, Bengaluru, etc.
By Train: Jammu Tawi Railway Station (290 km); travel via road through Srinagar
By Road: Connected via NH-44; regular taxis and buses from Srinagar

📍 Places to Visit Nearby

  • Srinagar – Dal Lake, houseboats, Mughal Gardens (50 km)
  • Pahalgam – Scenic valley for river rafting and treks (140 km)
  • Sonamarg – Glaciers and alpine beauty (125 km)
  • Yusmarg – Quiet retreat in pine forests (95 km)
  • Kupwara – Untouched landscapes for offbeat explorers (130 km)
