Explore the Royal Legacy of Agra

Home of the Taj Mahal and Majestic Mughal Glory

🏰 A Journey Through Mughal Majesty

Located along the Yamuna River in Uttar Pradesh, Agra is a historic city celebrated for its iconic Taj Mahal. Rich with Mughal architecture, vibrant markets, and timeless stories, it remains a crown jewel in India’s cultural heritage.

🌟 Why Visit Agra?

From sunrise at the Taj Mahal to shopping marble crafts and tasting Mughlai delights—Agra offers a complete experience of India’s royal past and local charm. A trip between October and March offers the best climate and unforgettable views.

📍 Top Attractions in Agra

  • Taj Mahal – Symbol of eternal love and Mughal brilliance
  • Agra Fort – A UNESCO World Heritage Site of royal legacy
  • Fatehpur Sikri – Ghost city of red sandstone beauty
  • Itmad-ud-Daulah – The intricate “Baby Taj”
  • Mehtab Bagh – Sunset views across the Yamuna
  • Akbar’s Tomb – A stunning mix of Indo-Islamic architecture

🎒 Things to Do in Agra

  • Witness sunrise at the Taj Mahal for a magical moment
  • Take a heritage walk through old lanes and markets
  • Try kebabs, biryanis, and Petha at Mughlai eateries
  • Enjoy a boat ride along the Yamuna River
  • Attend the light & sound show at Agra Fort
  • Watch Mohabbat-e-Taj – a musical at Kalakriti

📅 Best Time to Visit

October to March is perfect for exploring Agra’s monuments in pleasant weather. Mornings during winter offer photogenic fog-lit scenes at the Taj Mahal.

🌦️ Agra Weather Guide

Winter (Oct–Mar): Cool and ideal (8°C–25°C)
Summer (Apr–Jun): Hot and dry (up to 45°C)
Monsoon (Jul–Sep): Humid with light rain, fewer crowds

🎉 Festivals & Events

  • Taj Mahotsav (Feb): 10-day cultural fair near Taj
  • Ram Barat: A grand reenactment of Lord Ram’s wedding
  • Diwali & Eid: Streets glow with festive cheer

🛤️ How to Reach Agra

By Air: Kheria Airport (domestic), Delhi Airport (international – 220 km)
By Train: Agra Cantt, Agra Fort & Raja Ki Mandi stations. Fast trains like Shatabdi, Gatimaan available.
By Road: Yamuna Expressway from Delhi (3–4 hrs). Regular buses and taxis operate from nearby cities.

🏞️ Places Near Agra

  • Fatehpur Sikri (40 km) – Mughal heritage and architecture
  • Mathura & Vrindavan (60–70 km) – Birthplace of Lord Krishna
  • Bharatpur (55 km) – Birdwatching paradise at Keoladeo Park
  • Gwalior (120 km) – Forts, palaces, and royal legacy
  • Chambal Safari (80 km) – Boat rides & wildlife spotting
