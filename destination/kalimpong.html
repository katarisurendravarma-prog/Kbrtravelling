Kalimpong – A Hill Station of Serenity & Heritage

Where hills whisper stories of culture and calm

🌿 About Kalimpong

Located in northern West Bengal, Kalimpong is a peaceful hill town known for vibrant flower markets and heritage institutions. It was once a trade hub between India and Tibet and is now a scenic cultural getaway.

🏞️ Top Places to Visit

  • Deolo Hill: The highest point in Kalimpong offering views and adventure sports.
  • Cactus Nursery: Showcasing rare exotic plant species and export varieties.
  • Durpin Dara Hill: Views of the Himalayas and Teesta Valley near Zang Dhok Palri Monastery.

📅 Best Time to Visit

Kalimpong is pleasant year-round, but summer and winter offer the best experiences for sightseeing and chilly mountain air.

🎒 Things to Do

  • Explore Morgan House: A British-era haunted mansion
  • Visit Lepcha Museum: Learn about tribal culture and traditions

🚗 How to Reach

By Road: Connected to Bagdogra (79 km), Siliguri (70 km), Gangtok (75 km), and Darjeeling (50 km).

🚉 Nearest Railway Station & Buses

NJP (New Jalpaiguri) – 71 km away. Buses available from Siliguri, Kolkata, Gangtok, and Darjeeling.

✈️ Nearest Airport

Bagdogra Airport – 79 km away with flights from Kolkata, Delhi, and Guwahati.
