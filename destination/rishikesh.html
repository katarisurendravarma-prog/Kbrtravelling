Rejuvenate Your Soul in Rishikesh

Yoga, River Rafting, Spirituality & Scenic Beauty – All in One Place

🧘 Overview of Rishikesh

Rishikesh is a tranquil town in Uttarakhand nestled on the banks of the sacred Ganges River and surrounded by the foothills of the Himalayas. Known as the "Yoga Capital of the World," it offers spiritual serenity, adventure, and a peaceful retreat from city life.

🌊 Why Visit Rishikesh?

Perfect for spiritual seekers, adventure lovers, and nature enthusiasts – Rishikesh combines Ganga Aarti, meditation, river rafting, and stunning Himalayan views into one unforgettable experience.

📍 Best Places to Visit

  • Triveni Ghat – Iconic Ganga Aarti and peaceful spiritual ambiance
  • Laxman Jhula – Historic suspension bridge with scenic river views
  • Neelkanth Mahadev Temple – Sacred Shiva temple surrounded by forest
  • Beatles Ashram – Meditative ruins with colorful graffiti and history

⛅ Best Time to Visit

Winter (Oct–Mar): Best season for travel, yoga, and rafting
Summer (Apr–Jun): Hot and dry; early mornings best for activities
Monsoon (Jul–Sep): Lush landscapes but risk of landslides

🎉 Festivals & Events

  • International Yoga Festival (March) – Global gathering of yogis
  • Ganga Dussehra (May/June) – Celebrating the descent of the holy river
  • Makar Sankranti (January) – Festivities along the Ganges with kites and rituals

🎒 Things to Do in Rishikesh

  • River Rafting – Thrilling rapids in the Ganges
  • Ganga Aarti – Attend the magical evening prayers at Triveni Ghat
  • Camping – Stay in riverside tents with bonfires and music
  • Yoga & Meditation – Join sessions at renowned ashrams
  • Bungee Jumping – Try India’s highest bungee at Mohan Chatti

🚉 How to Reach Rishikesh

By Air: Jolly Grant Airport (20 km) in Dehradun connects to major cities
By Train: Haridwar Junction (25 km) – Frequent trains from Delhi, Mumbai, Varanasi
By Road: NH 58 links Rishikesh to Delhi, Haridwar, and Dehradun

🌄 Places to Visit Nearby

  • Haridwar (25 km) – Sacred city famous for its Ganga Aarti
  • Dehradun (45 km) – Peaceful capital city with colonial charm
  • Mussoorie (80 km) – Hill station perfect for weekend escapes
  • Devprayag (70 km) – Confluence of Alaknanda and Bhagirathi rivers
