The City of Lakes – Udaipur

Palaces, sunsets, and timeless romance on the lakes of Rajasthan

🏞️ Overview of Udaipur

Udaipur, often called the "City of Lakes," is a romantic and scenic destination nestled in the Aravalli Hills. With regal palaces, tranquil waters, and colorful streets, it offers a timeless experience for travelers looking for history, culture, or love.

🎨 Why Visit Udaipur?

To witness the grandeur of Rajasthani royalty, ride a boat over Lake Pichola, enjoy cultural dance performances, and experience sunset views from the Sajjangarh Palace—Udaipur is Rajasthan’s crown jewel for romantic and cultural getaways.

📸 Best Places to Visit

  • City Palace – Marvel of Rajput architecture with lake views
  • Lake Pichola – Sunset boat rides with views of palaces
  • Jag Mandir – Historic island palace with beautiful domes
  • Sajjangarh (Monsoon Palace) – Hilltop spot for city panoramas
  • Jagdish Temple – Ornate temple in the heart of Udaipur
  • Bagore Ki Haveli – Folk dance shows and cultural museum

⛅ Best Time to Visit

Winter (Oct–Mar): Ideal for sightseeing and outdoor events
Summer (Apr–Jun): Hot but romantic evenings
Monsoon (Jul–Sep): Green surroundings and lesser crowds

🎊 Festivals & Events

  • Mewar Festival – Cultural celebration of spring
  • Shilpgram Fair – Handicrafts and folk performances
  • Diwali – Lakes and palaces glow with lights

🎒 Things to Do in Udaipur

  • Boat ride on Lake Pichola
  • Explore City Palace complex
  • Watch cultural shows at Bagore Ki Haveli
  • Shop at Hathi Pol for traditional crafts
  • Dine at a rooftop café overlooking the lake

🛬 How to Reach Udaipur

By Air: Maharana Pratap Airport (22 km) – connected to Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore
By Train: Direct trains from Delhi, Jaipur, Ahmedabad (Mewar Express, Chetak Express)
By Road: Connected via NH-8/NH-76 to Jaipur (6 hrs), Delhi (10 hrs), Ahmedabad (4.5 hrs)

📍 Places to Visit Nearby

  • Mount Abu (165 km) – Hill station with Dilwara Temples and Nakki Lake
  • Kumbhalgarh Fort (85 km) – Famous for its long fort wall and panoramic views
  • Chittorgarh (120 km) – Grand fort known for tales of Rajput valor
  • Ranakpur (90 km) – Renowned for intricate Jain temples
  • Eklingji Temple (22 km) – Sacred Shiva temple with historic architecture
