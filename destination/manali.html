Escape to the Hills of Manali

Snowy peaks, adventurous trails, and soulful charm await you in Himachal’s gem

🏔️ Overview of Manali

Manali, nestled in Himachal Pradesh, is a favorite all-season destination offering breathtaking landscapes, cozy cafes, thrilling adventure sports, and a rich mix of cultures. Whether you seek snow, solitude, or a scenic escape—Manali has it all.

✨ Why Visit Manali?

From skiing in Solang Valley to soaking in Vashisht hot springs, Manali is the perfect mountain getaway with activities for honeymooners, families, backpackers, and thrill-seekers alike.

📸 Best Places to Visit

  • Hadimba Temple – Surrounded by cedar forests, with distinct wooden architecture
  • Solang Valley – Adventure hub for skiing, paragliding, and more
  • Rohtang Pass – Breathtaking views, open seasonally May–Nov
  • Old Manali – Rustic charm, lively cafes, art, and shopping
  • Manu Temple – Dedicated to sage Manu with tranquil mountain ambiance

⛅ Best Time to Visit

Summer (Mar–Jun): Ideal for sightseeing, river rafting, and paragliding
Monsoon (Jul–Sep): Lush landscapes, but landslide-prone
Winter (Oct–Feb): Snowy delight with skiing and romantic vibes

🎊 Festivals to Experience

  • Winter Carnival – Culture, dance, and skiing contests (January)
  • Dussehra – Traditional celebrations in nearby Kullu (October)
  • Hadimba Devi Fair – Local rituals and music (May)

🎒 Things to Do in Manali

  • Rafting in Beas River
  • Trek to Jogini Falls
  • Soak in Vashisht Hot Springs
  • Explore Naggar Castle
  • Shop and chill in Old Manali

🛬 How to Reach Manali

By Air: Bhuntar Airport (50 km); connected via taxis
By Train: Joginder Nagar (145 km) or Chandigarh for wider access
By Road: Regular buses/taxis from Delhi, Chandigarh, Shimla; scenic self-drive route

📍 Places to Visit Nearby

  • Solang Valley – Adventure playground
  • Rohtang Pass – Iconic snow-filled pass
  • Naggar Castle – Historic fort and art gallery
  • Kullu – River rafting, temples, and handicrafts
