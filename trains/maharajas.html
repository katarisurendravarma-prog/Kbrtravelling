🚆 A Palace on Rails

With opulent interiors, personalized hospitality, fine dining, and curated heritage excursions, the Maharajas’ Express recreates the grandeur of royal travel from India’s golden era.

📜 About the Train

Launched in 2010 by IRCTC, the Maharajas’ Express was designed to reflect the extravagant lifestyle of India’s royalty. The train features themed suites, observation lounges, and bars to ensure an unforgettable journey through India’s cultural heartland.

🏆 Global Recognition

This majestic train has consistently earned accolades as the “World’s Leading Luxury Train,” making it a favorite among elite travelers and cultural explorers worldwide.
