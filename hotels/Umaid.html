Perched atop Chittar Hill, Umaid Bhawan offers panoramic views of the Blue City, golden dunes, and the Mehrangarh Fort. It boasts 70 lavish rooms and suites amidst 26 acres of lush gardens, complete with the peacock-lined Zodiac Pool.

The interiors, made of the same palm court marble used in the Taj Mahal, are adorned with murals by Polish artist Stefan Norblin and glitter with timeless luxury and elegance.
