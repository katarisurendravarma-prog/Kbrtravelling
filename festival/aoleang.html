Experience the springtime spirit of Nagaland with the Konyak tribe

📍 Destinations Covered

Delhi – Guwahati – Kaziranga – Nameri – Dirang – Tawang – Bomdila – Ziro – Majuli – Mon – Dibrugarh – Delhi

🗓️ Duration

18 Days / 17 Nights

🎉 Overview

This immersive itinerary takes you from Delhi to the remote highlands and valleys of Northeast India. From rhino spotting in Kaziranga to tribal dances in Mon, experience nature, spirituality, and cultural wonders like never before.

📝 Detailed Itinerary Highlights

Days 1–2: Delhi – Guwahati – Kaziranga

Arrive in Delhi and fly to Guwahati. Enjoy safaris in Kaziranga National Park.

Day 3–4: Nameri & Dirang

Walk through Nameri Park’s forests and continue to Dirang via the Orchidarium.

Day 5–7: Tawang

Cross the Sela Pass to Tawang. Visit Tawang Monastery, Indo-China War Memorial, and Gyangong Anigompa.

Day 8–9: Bomdila – Tezpur

Explore Bomdila’s market and monastery. Head to Tezpur and visit Mahabhairab Temple.

Day 10–11: Ziro

Meet the Apatani tribe and explore the cultural heritage of Old Ziro.

Day 12–13: Majuli & Sivasagar

Visit Majuli’s satras and ferry to Sivasagar to explore Ahom-era architecture.

Day 14–16: Mon & Aoleang Festival

Join the Konyak tribe’s vibrant Aoleang celebration in Longwa and Shangnyu villages.

Day 17–18: Dibrugarh – Delhi

Visit tea gardens in Assam before flying back to Delhi.
